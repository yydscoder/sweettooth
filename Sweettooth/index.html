Our Philosophy

Initially started off as health reasons, wanting to find low-calorie options. But digging into ingredients lists revealed that while healthy ice cream is possible, it's often just a scam - injecting air and cheap sugar powders to inflate volume.

This journey made us realize that it's not possible to ONLY eat healthy - life's too boring for that. Just make sure whatever you put into your mouth is the best you can get to make your calories worth it!

We believe in Japan's Shun (旬) culture - using ingredients when they're in season. Living in SEA gives us access to fresh fruits year-round. We don't serve Musang King all year long because we won't compromise on consistency, taste, and freshness.

Look forward to our Mangosteen sorbet - something the Malaysian market isn't ready for but will soon appreciate!
