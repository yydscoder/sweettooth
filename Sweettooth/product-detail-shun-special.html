March 2026 brings us the fragrant Harum Manis mango at the cusp of its season — intensely sweet, deeply aromatic. We've paired it with a black sesame ripple for contrast: earthy, nutty, and darkly beautiful against the golden mango. A limited run. Once it's gone, it's gone.

The Philosophy of Shun (旬)

旬

In Japan, shun refers to the precise peak season of an ingredient — the short window when its flavor, aroma, and nutrition all align perfectly. Eating shun is not merely about freshness; it's a philosophy of attentiveness, of meeting nature where it is.

We named this flavor after that philosophy because it captures exactly what we are trying to do: pay attention to what the season is offering, and honor it in the most delicious way we know how.
