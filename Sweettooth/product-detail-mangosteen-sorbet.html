Mangosteen Sorbet

Dairy-Free Vegan Seasonal Limited

A seasonal delicacy crafted exclusively during peak mangosteen season. We source only the finest Queen of Fruits from local Malaysian farms, capturing that unmistakable sweet-tart perfume in every spoonful. No artificial flavors, no concentrates — just pure, unadulterated fruit bliss.

RM 15 per 100ml

Flavor Profile

Taste

Sweet, floral, and lightly tart — distinctly Southeast Asian with a clean, bright finish

Texture

Light, silky sorbet — refreshingly icy with a smooth melt. Dairy-free and guilt-free

Aroma

Delicately floral with tropical fruit notes that open as it warms on the palate

Ingredients

Fresh Malaysian mangosteens, cane sugar, water, lemon juice

Why Mangosteen?

Known as the "Queen of Fruits" in Southeast Asia, the mangosteen has a brief seasonal window that makes it all the more precious. Its creamy white segments carry a perfume unlike any other fruit — sweet and tart simultaneously, with floral undertones that seem to bloom as you eat. We wait all year for this season. We hope you'll understand why when you taste it.
