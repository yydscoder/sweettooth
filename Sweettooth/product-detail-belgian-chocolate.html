Ingredients

Why Callebaut?

Founded in 1911 in Wieze, Belgium, Callebaut has been the benchmark for premium chocolate for over a century. Their single-origin and blended couvertures are used by the world's finest pastry kitchens because they deliver consistent, complex, deeply satisfying chocolate flavor. We chose the 811 Dark — 70.5% cacao — for its balance of intensity, slight bitterness, and extraordinary smoothness when churned into gelato.
